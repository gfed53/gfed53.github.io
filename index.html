About Me

I'm an aspiring web developer, relatively new to the wonderful world of coding and programming in general. I've always had an interest in technology of all avenues, and since around October of 2014 have started to learn various languages on my own.

I've been working as a focus group recruiter for over 4 years now (yikes...), trying to use it as a stepping stone to getting into the area of market research. I didn't think it was possible to remain on a stepping stone for so long without falling into the water. Luckily, I was able to grab ahold of the helicopter rope ladder that is web development. We shall see where that takes me..

Put generally, I'm just a lover of all things interesting in life. If it's different and it stands out, I'm sure I can find some beauty in it, or at the very least find something to ponder over.

What's important to me is to be successful in whatever I do, while still maintaining a balance of life, eventually reaching the point where I can enjoy work as if it were a hobby.
