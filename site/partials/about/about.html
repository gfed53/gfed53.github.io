What I Do

Front end web developer with rapid-level learning abilities, looking for exciting and fulfilling job opportunies.

I am a front end web developer looking to gain experience in the field. I've always had an interest in technology of all avenues, and since around October of 2014 have started to learn various languages on my own.

I then studied front end web development through Thinkful's Front End Career Path. I went from learning the fundamental building blocks of HTML/CSS, version control of GIT, and core concepts of Javascript, to responsive design/mobile-first web development, CSS preprocessors, some game development, and eventually tackling the extensive framework of Angular, all the while learning to use various libraries to make life as a web developer easier and more enjoyable.

What I hope to achieve in the near future is, as mentioned before, experience in the field. I'm at that stage where I've cultivated a fairly great deal of knowledge and have applied it, but not yet in a professional setting. With experience I'll be able to hone my skills as well as gauge my strengths and weaknesses, and improve as a web developer. I also try to branch out when I can and explore other areas of programming, and IT in general. It'd be great to be a full-stack developer one day.
