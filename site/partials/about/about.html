Who I Am

Some of my hobbies include playing guitar, listening to obscure music and trying to find people who actually have heard of it, (mostly retro) gaming, being somewhat of a YouTube junkie (shamelessly), and exercising religiously.

What I Do

I am a junior-level front end web developer look to gain experience in the field. I've always had an interest in technology of all avenues, and since around October of 2014 have started to learn various languages on my own.

I then studied front end web development through Thinkful's Front End Career Path. I went from learning the fundamental building blocks of HTML/CSS, version control of GIT, core concepts of Javascript, to responsive design/mobile-first web development, CSS preprocessors, some game development, and eventually tackling the extensive framework of Angular, all the while learning to use various libraries to make life as a web developer easier and more enjoyable.
